About

Ever since their founding, the Harvard Art Museums — have been dedicated to advancing and supporting learning at Harvard University, in the local community, and around the world. The museums havevplayed a leading role in the development of art history, conservation, and conservation science, and in the evolution of the art museum as an institution.
